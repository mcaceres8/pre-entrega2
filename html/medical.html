English for Medical Purposes

Materials Used & Books

These books are ideal for pre-work, and work students and also professionals, studying at upper-intermediate to advanced level, who will need to use English in work situations. It is also suitable for doctors and other health professionals who plan to work in English-speaking countries. By using them, we will develop the vocabulary, language, and skills that students need to read and understand medical texts, to be successful in medical exams, and to communicate effectively and accurately with patients and colleagues.
